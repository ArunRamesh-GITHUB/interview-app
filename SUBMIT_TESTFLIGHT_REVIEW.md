# How to Submit to TestFlight for Review (Beta App Review)

## Why You Need This

Even though your app is already in TestFlight, **In-App Purchases need the app to be submitted for Beta App Review** to be available in TestFlight Sandbox.

## Steps to Submit for Beta App Review

### Step 1: Go to App Store Connect

1. Go to [App Store Connect](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com)
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Click on **"My Apps"**
4. Select your app: **"NailIT Interview Prep"**

### Step 2: Navigate to TestFlight

1. Click on **"TestFlight"** tab (top navigation)
2. You should see your build listed

### Step 3: Submit for Beta App Review

1. **Find your build** in the list
2. **Click on the build** to open details
3. Look for **"Submit for Review"** button (usually at the top right)
4. **OR** if you see **"Add to External Testing"** or **"Add to Internal Testing"**:
   - Click on it
   - This will trigger Beta App Review

### Step 4: Fill Out Beta App Review Information

When submitting, you'll need to provide:

1. **Beta App Review Information:**
   - **Contact Information**: Your email/phone
   - **Demo Account** (if needed): Credentials for testing
   - **Notes**: Brief description of what the app does
   - **Sign in required?**: Yes/No
   - **Additional Information**: Any notes for reviewers

2. **In-App Purchases:**
   - Make sure all 4 products are listed
   - They should be included automatically

3. **Review Notes:**
   - Example: "This is a TestFlight beta build for internal testing. The app allows users to practice interview questions and purchase token packs for additional practice sessions."

### Step 5: Submit

1. **Review all information**
2. **Click "Submit for Review"**
3. **Wait for processing** (usually 24-48 hours, but can be faster)

## Alternative: Add to External Testing

If you don't see "Submit for Review", try:

1. **TestFlight tab** → **External Testing** (or **Internal Testing**)
2. **Click "+" to create new group** (if needed)
3. **Add your build** to the testing group
4. **Submit for Beta App Review** when prompted

## What Happens After Submission

1. **App enters "Waiting for Review"** status
2. **Apple reviews the app** (usually 24-48 hours)
3. **Once approved**, In-App Purchases become available in TestFlight
4. **You'll get an email** when review is complete

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] App is uploaded to TestFlight ✅ (you have this)
- [ ] Go to TestFlight tab in App Store Connect
- [ ] Click on your build
- [ ] Click "Submit for Review" or "Add to External Testing"
- [ ] Fill out Beta App Review Information
- [ ] Include In-App Purchases in submission
- [ ] Submit and wait for review

## After Review is Approved

Once approved:
1. **In-App Purchases will be available** in TestFlight Sandbox
2. **Products should load** when you open the buy screen
3. **You can test purchases** with Sandbox test account

---

**Note:** Even for TestFlight, Apple requires Beta App Review for apps with In-App Purchases. This is normal and necessary for IAP to work in TestFlight.
